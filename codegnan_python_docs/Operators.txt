Operators

-> There are 7 types 

1. Arithmetic Operators -> +,-,*,//,/,% 

2.Assignment Operators -> +=,-=,*=,//=,/=

3.Comparison Operators -> <,>,<=,>=,==,!=

4.Logical operators -> and, or, not

5.Identify Operators -> is, is not 

6.Membership operators -> in, not in

7.Bitwise operators -> &,|,~,^
